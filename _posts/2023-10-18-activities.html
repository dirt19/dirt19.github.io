What else have I been doing whilst searching for employment?

My usual daily schedule is currently:

  • 6am : morning reading, skim news for current events
  • 8am - 9am : Review any recruiter correspondence that came in
  • 9am - 12pm : Review job postings (e.g. LinkedIn, Indeed, company-of-interest careers site)
  • 12pm - 1pm : lunch, walk
  • 1pm - 3pm : Skills Development and Review
  • 3pm - 4pm : Review any additional correspondence that may have come in (emails, LinkedIn messages, etc.)
  • 4pm - 5pm : Wrap up, check calendar for any planned interviews, phone screens, or other activities and events
